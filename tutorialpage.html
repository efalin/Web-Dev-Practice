Leather Armor Tutorial

Learn how to cover EVA foam armor with leather

Tutorial Steps

(1) You'll want to start out by taking your piece of pre-made foam armor, and cutting out a piece of leather the same shape, but slightly bigger. (2) From there, apply contact cement to the front of the foam and the back of the leather, letting it dry and then firmly pressing the two pieces together. (3) Use scissors to cut small notches in the edges of the leather to help it fold over without any wrinkles, and then fold the excess edges of leather over the bottom of the armor and glue them down. (4) And you're done! You have a nice, realistic piece of leather armor for your cosplay.
